I love learning modern computer technology because I think it is a combination of art and science. I am a huge fan of online interactive learning. Treehouse, Coursera, and Codecademy are my favourates. I have a solid understanding of CS fundamentals, data structures and algorithms. I really enjoy developing "beautiful" software solutions.
