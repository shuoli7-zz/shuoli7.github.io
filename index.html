MORE ABOUT ME?

I was born and raised in a very beautiful city, Dalian in the northeastern China. When I was a kid, I dreamed to be a wizard like Harry Potter because I thought magic is beautiful and it also makes me be able to have an impact on other people's lives. When I grew up, I didn't become a wizard, instead I became an engineer. I really enjoy my study, because I think engineering is the closest thing to the magic in the real life. Beautiful and Powerful!

I studied my Bachelor of Science with Honors in Electrical Engineering at UIUC, with a minor in Computer Science. During my undergraduate years, my study was focused on digital systems design. From studying simple logic gates to designing CPU, from writing hello world.c to coding video games, I was excited and satisfied about my progress. However, with the growth of my knowledge, I saw a bigger world. I realized that there are still many things I need to know and with a strong technical background, an artistic mind, hunger for innovation and caring for humanities, there are ways I could make an impact on. So I decided to further my study in the master program at ECE Illinois.

My graduate study at UIUC has been exciting and rewarding. The classes on web/mobile applications development provided me with valuable theories, principles, tools and skills as an application developer. I also joined in Prof. Jose Schutt-Aine's research group as a graduate researcher. Working with my brilliant advisor and smart groupmates in research projects sharpened my collaboration and communication skills, also inspired me in many ways.

Besides academic study and research, I also participated in teaching activities. I have been teaching a junior-level course, ECE385 Digital Systems Laboratory since August 2013. Making an impact on other students' studies and helping them make progress with my knowledge make me feel enjoyable. In my last semester at UIUC, I plan to take some interesting courses on UI Design, Web Programming, and AI to improve my skills. After my graduation, I wish to work on some exciting and creative projects as an application developer/software engineer.

My other hobbies include sports, movies, cooking and travelling. I'm a crazy basketball and soccer fan. Los Angeles Lakers and Real Madrid are my favourite sport teams. My idols are Kobe Bryant and Cristiano Ronaldo. I am their huge fan because I think we share the common characters, which are competitive, passionate, self-motivated and ambitious to do better. In my leisure time, I enjoy cooking for my family and friends.Traveling and exploring this amazing world with them also make me very happy.
